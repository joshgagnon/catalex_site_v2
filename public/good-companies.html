Why use Good Companies?

Companies Register

Import a company’s details and share history from the New Zealand companies register.  Bulk import available. 

Share Register

Every New Zealand company must have a share register, but many do not.  With Good Companies, in a few clicks your company can have a share register that is compliant with section 87 of the Companies Act 1993.

Interests Register

Create and maintain a register of the interests of the company’s directors as required by section 189 of the Companies Act 1993.

Transactions

Create and manage share transactions - issue, transfer, redeem, repurchase, consolidate, and sub-divide shares in accordance with the Companies Act 1993.

Manage Shares and Shareholders

Create and assign share classes, add or remove rights and limitations attached to shares, and manage shareholder information.

Templates

Create template documents, including share transfer forms and director resolutions, and export them in PDF, docx or odt format.
